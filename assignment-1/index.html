Wall

by Good Kid

Album Aesthetic

Good Kid’s visual identity frequently uses hand-drawn textures, muted urban colours, and playful imperfections. The artwork features illustrated cityscapes, layered structures, and warm sunset gradients. This aesthetic reinforces the emotional honesty of their music while maintaining a youthful indie tone.

About the Song

“Wall” explores emotional distance and the frustration of feeling blocked from meaningful connection. The song’s driving rhythm and introspective lyrics mirror the tension between isolation and the desire to be understood.

About the Artist

Good Kid is an indie rock band known for blending energetic, guitar-driven melodies with reflective storytelling. Their music often pairs upbeat arrangements with themes of anxiety, identity, and emotional distance.
