Wall

by Good Kid

About the Song

“Wall” explores emotional distance, frustration, and the feeling of being unheard. The song’s driving rhythm and introspective lyrics capture the tension between wanting connection and feeling blocked by invisible barriers.

About the Artist

Good Kid is an indie rock band known for blending energetic melodies with reflective storytelling. Their music often balances upbeat instrumentation with themes of anxiety, growth, and self-awareness.
